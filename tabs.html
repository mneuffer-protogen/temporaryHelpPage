VR Experience Setup

  1. Put on your VR headset
    Ensure your headset is properly adjusted and comfortable on your head
  2. Check controller battery
    Make sure your VR controllers are charged and properly connected
  3. Clear your play area
    Remove any obstacles and ensure you have adequate space to move around safely
  4. Launch the VR store
    Open your VR headset's app store or game library interface
  5. Search and install Beat Saber
    Find Beat Saber in the store and follow the installation prompts
