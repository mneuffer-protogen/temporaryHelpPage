VR Experience Setup

  1. Download The ESAMTAC VR Taining Lab App
    Option 1: In Browser, open ESAMTAC VR Training Lab horizon store page. Log in with same account as headset. Click "Get" and "Install" Option 2: Put on your headset, open the horizon store, search ESAMTAC, download "ESAMTAC VR Training Lab"
    2. Download VR Training Lab App
  2. Open ESAMTAC VR Training Lab
    Once downloaded, open the ESAMTAC VR Training Lab app in the headset.
  3. Sign In
    In the VR Training Lab, enter your email registered to the course

Desktop Setup Guide

  1. Check system requirements
    Ensure your computer meets the minimum hardware specifications for the application
  2. Download the installer
    Visit the official website and download the latest version of the software
  3. Run the installation
    Execute the downloaded installer and follow the setup wizard instructions
  4. Open the application
    Launch the ESAMTAC Virtual Training Lab app and Log-In using your email registered for the course.
